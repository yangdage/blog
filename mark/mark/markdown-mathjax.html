$x^{y^2}=(1+{\rm e}^2)^{-2xy}$

$\int_0^1 x^2 {\rm d}x$

极限运算

$\lim_{n \rightarrow +\infty} \frac{1}{n(n+1)}$

$\sum_{i=0}^n \frac{1}{i^2}$

$\prod_{i=0}^n \frac{1}{i^2}$

$\cos 2\theta = \cos^2 \theta - \sin^2 \theta = 2 \cos^2 \theta - 1$
